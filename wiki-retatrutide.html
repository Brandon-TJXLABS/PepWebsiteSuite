Retatrutide

Chemical reference and research-literature summary for Retatrutide — for laboratory use only.

Overview

Retatrutide (LY3437943) is a synthetic modified peptide, sold here as a 10mg vial of lyophilized powder. It is a 39-amino-acid peptide built on a GIP backbone, with a fatty diacid conjugation for extended stability, and is studied in the literature as a combined agonist across the GIP, GLP-1, and glucagon receptors.
