Bacteriostatic Water

Chemical reference for Bacteriostatic Water — for laboratory use only.

Overview

Bacteriostatic Water is a sterile aqueous diluent, sold here as a 3mL vial containing 0.9% benzyl alcohol as an antimicrobial preservative. In laboratory settings it is used as a solvent to bring lyophilized peptide powders into solution. It is not itself a peptide or research compound under study — this page documents the chemical identity of its preservative component.
