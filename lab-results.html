How testing works

Our verification process

Step 1

A sample from every production batch is sent to an independent lab, unaffiliated with our suppliers.

Step 2

HPLC testing confirms purity and identity. Endotoxin levels are measured separately.

Step 3

Results are published here before the batch is listed for sale. Failed batches are discarded, not sold.
