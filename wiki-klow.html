Research reference

KLOW

Chemical reference and research-literature summary for the KLOW blend — for laboratory use only.

Overview

KLOW is a multi-compound research blend, sold here as an 80mg vial of lyophilized powder combining four individually studied peptides: BPC-157 (10mg), TB-500 (10mg), GHK-Cu (50mg), and KPV (10mg). Each component has its own chemical identity and body of preclinical literature, summarized below rather than as a single compound.

Studied in the research literature for

General topic areas each component appears in published preclinical/animal research — not a statement of confirmed outcomes, and not applicable to human use.

  • BPC-157: gastrointestinal and musculoskeletal tissue-repair models, angiogenesis studies
  • TB-500: cell-migration and actin-regulation studies, tissue-repair models
  • GHK-Cu: wound-healing, collagen-synthesis, and antioxidant enzyme studies
  • KPV: intestinal-inflammation and skin-inflammation models

Storage & handling

  • Store lyophilized (powder) vials at -20°C, protected from light.
  • Once reconstituted for laboratory use, keep refrigerated at 2–8°C.
  • Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles, which can degrade peptide integrity.
  • Handle in accordance with your institution's standard laboratory safety practices.
