About Aciona

Built for people who read the COA before they read the label.

We started Aciona because too much of the research peptide market runs on trust alone. We think verification should come standard, not as an upsell.

Our approach

What we actually do

01 — Sourcing

Every supplier goes through a documented vetting process before a single batch reaches our shelf.

02 — Testing

Independent third-party labs verify purity and identity on every batch — not spot checks, every batch.

03 — Publishing

Results go on the site before the product goes on sale. If a batch fails, it doesn't ship.

Why Aciona exists

Verification isn't a feature. It's the whole point.

Most research suppliers ask you to take their word for it. We built Aciona around the opposite idea — every batch gets tested before it gets sold, every result gets published before you buy, and nothing about that process is optional or upsold.

Uncompromising quality

Every batch is independently tested before it's listed — not sampled occasionally, not tested after the fact. If a batch doesn't pass, it doesn't go on sale.

Full transparency

Every Certificate of Analysis is published on this site, not sent on request. You can check purity and identity before you ever add something to your cart.

Straightforward service

Real answers from real people — no scripts, no runaround. If something's wrong with an order, we fix it.

Compliance & responsibility

Every product is sold strictly for laboratory research use, in line with the terms every customer agrees to before checkout — no exceptions, no gray areas.

Research use only. Aciona supplies compounds for laboratory research purposes only. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, and no products are intended for human or animal consumption.
